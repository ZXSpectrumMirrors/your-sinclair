1st December 2009 : The countdown has ended, and December has begun. Later on this month will see the release of the documentary Christmas Special, but right now I want to give ZXmas a jump start with the launch of my brand new Advent Calendar!
    Each day there's a page full of festive Speccy memories from my own Speccy Christmases, together with Vox Pops from players in the Speccy scene both past and present where they share their own memories.
    I've also raided YouTube for old television and other clips, and there's a rather eclectic mix of clips in the Advent Calendar as a result.
    And to round things off, there are also free Virtual Advent Calendar Chocolates - completely genuine cheap tacky synthetic chocolates that most Advent Calendars had when I grew up, and these have the added bonus of being completely fat-free!
    I hope you enjoy this ocean of nostalgia to wallow in, and keep an eye out for the documentary Christmas Special later this month!

27th June 2009 : A nice little YS94 bonus for you - the archives I was sent by the YS94 team included the original DTP files used to print the supplement, and Andrew Rollings has converted them to PDF, and they look quite fab indeed. There's something striking about seeing these pages in high definition onscreen. Take a look at the YS94 page for download links.
25th June 2009 : THE 2004 YOUR SINCLAIR SPECIAL IS NOW ONLINE!
    A few weeks ago I approached Imagine Publishing, publishers of Retro Gamer, about putting their 32-page YS special from 2004 online, and not only did Damian Butt (Imagine MD) give the go ahead, but he and the YS94 team (Dan Whitehead, Martyn Carroll, Colin Woodcock and Shaun Bebbington) dug into their archives and sent me a shedload of files used to create the original supplement.
    Including some unreleased stuff!
    So today I present to you YS94 - the 2004 Special, pretty much completely online, with one unpublished article, two bonus pages, plus a re-release of the analysis I wrote back in 2004 when it was released.
    Huge huge thanks to the YS94 Team and Imagine for everything they've done - it's been a very fun June as a result.
    Enjoy!
