Your Computer

January 1988
Issue 25

 
View/download
cover tape
YS scoops the other magazines by having an exclusive Monty Mole game Moley Christmas on the cover tape.
    An astonishing eleven Megagames in this issue.
    Board game feature Dicing With Death.
    Novelty Christmas presents feature.
    Hoping to follow-up on the forthcoming guaranteed success of OutRun, Road Blasters is the latest arcade licence to be acquired by US Gold.
    John Minson warns CRL against being so jubilant about the press publicity given to them when their latest game, Jack The Ripper, gets an 18 certificate from the BBFC. He forsees that all computer games will eventually have to get compulsory ratings. A few generations of computers on, he isn't far wrong...
