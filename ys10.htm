Your Computer

October 1986
Issue 10

Gargoyle's new 'Faster than Light' label advertises Lightforce and Shockway Rider.
    Hard Facts special feature on the internal workings of the new Spectrum 128k. According to YS 70,000 have been sold.
    6-page Elite feature with Hacking Program, Designer Program and Survival Guide.
    Many thanks to Jim Grimwood for supplying this entry!
    Elite reinforces its reputation for getting some of the best coin-op licences by acquiring Space Harrier.
    Max Phillips tackles game hackers in Backlash.
