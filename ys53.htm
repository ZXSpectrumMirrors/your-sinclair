Another mediocre tape: Samurai (CRL, 7, April 1987) and a demo of Scramble Spirits (Grandslam).
    Irregular colum SAM Surgeon starts, and reveals plenty of up-coming software titles.
    YS starts it's "Complete YS Guides to..." series, this issue covering beat-'em-ups.
    YS Critics' Games of 1989 announced: first was Stunt Car Racer (Microprose), second Chase HQ (Ocean) and third Carrier Command (Rainbird).
