Speccy 128 released at £179.95 and reviewed - and is found to be less than 100% backwards compatible.
    Modem House sells the BTX 5000 Modem - a 1200/75 modem for £29.95.
    Arcade Dream April Fool fools rather a lot of people - including one rival magazine...

    The legendary YS badge is introduced!

    After being written, scapped, rewritten and scrapped again, Elite says that Scooby Doo is back on the release schedule.
