Welcome!
Every reader of Your Sinclair will probably have wondered what on earth they were going to read now that the Great Magazine had closed down. Over the years a wide range of fanzines have been produced by a dedicated fanbase. Almost certainly the most recent of these is YS3.

    YS3 was launched in 1999, designed to be the natural successor to Your Sinclair. (YS2 was a tapezine attached to the front of the last few issues of YS.) Universally accepted as a Good Thing by the Sinclair community, YS3 continued to delight and entertain its six and a half readers every month (or thereabouts) for about four years until the final issue in 2002.

    Now, we are proud to present the complete back issues archive. Thanks to Mr Nick Humphries, the archive is both browsable, searchable, and entirely downloadable. Sit back, relax, and indulge yourself with ramblings about toffee cream slices and scantily-clad girlies.
