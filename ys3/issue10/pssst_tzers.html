A Tragic Loss

(Posted on comp.sys.sinclair by 'Ralph Wiggum')

Re: Dangermouse: Double Trouble

I don't know how many people here heard of this game or played it but it is my sad duty to inform the Sinclair community that Martin Harris, one of the writers of this game, passed away recently. He wasn't very well and ended his own life from the car park he used to sit opposite every day at work.

He is greatly missed by his colleagues.

The reason I write this is because I remember seeing some site with info on authors of Spectrum games and what they do now and I thought that maybe this could get added to it. I suppose it's my way of saying goodbye.

(Martin Harris was also the author of Gold Rush and Snodgits. Our sincerest condolences to Mr Harris' family and friends.)

Ar-Har Jim-Lad!

It's come to our attention that certain websites have started hosting local "modified" copies of RetroSpec games. Whilst not mentioning any sites in particular, a RetroSpec spokesman said "Some of these files available on other sites have been modified to remove copyright messages etc. Some don't work properly or at all. It's a flagrant disregard for the hard work of the author(s). I urge all people wishing to play RetroSpec games to download them from our site - ensuring after-download support, lack of viruses and the latest versions." We then edited out all but one of the hyperlinks and corrected the spelling.
