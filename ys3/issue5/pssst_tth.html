Number Five - Dale Winton

Well, hello there! What's that? You want me to come out of the closet about my secret desires? Well, I'm not sure that the world is ready for that just yet... I'm sorry? Oh, you mean trains! Oh, well, that's another story entirely. Yes, I simply adore trains. When I've finished clearing up the mess after another episode of Supermarket Sweep, I enjoy nothing more than sneaking down to Paddington and gazing longingly at the engines. You know, when I was younger I always wanted to be an engine driver. It always seemed so romantic, travelling up and down the country, where-ever the timetable took me. Ah, things could have been so very different. Still, things aren't so bad now, eh? Although it would've been nice to get my hands on the driver's brake. Hey ho. What's that? The Spectrum? Yes, I used to have one of those. I had a ZX81, too, but it kept crashing whenever I joggled the RAM pack. I couldn't get enough of the old Speccy. Ah, nothing like the feel of rubber under your fingers to get you in the mood for a bit of Jet Set Willy, eh? Eh? Where'd they go?
