Issue Two - July 21, 1999

OPI : 520

    Hello Spec-chums!

    Crikey, back again for a second month. I was kind of expecting this whole thing to fail hopelessly (as, no doubt, more than a few of you). But no. Here we are again, with a whole new issue, even more packed with almost-entirely Spectrum-related material. This month we've (technically) had a bit more time to prepare (last month we had about two weeks to do everything, including the graphics), so there's a lot more material for you to peruse.
    This month we've also gone out of our way to caption almost every picture here. Those in the know call it ALT text - hold your mouse cursor steady over an image to read the caption.
    All in all, this ish looks like being a whole lot better than last month's (and last month's was brill. Well I thought it was, anyway.). Read on, and enjoy!

    Nathan Cross
