Joystick Jugglers
    Nathan Cross
    This month Nathan tried to install Linux on his PC. First he had to buy a new hard drive, because Windows wouldn't defrag his existing drive properly so that he could repartition it. Then he found that his dial-up connections wouldn't work. After a little hysterical screaming and swearing and threatening to bring all manner of violence into use against whatever bast wrote the Linux 'error messages', he swapped the hard drives back and rebooted Windows. Still, maybe next month, eh?
