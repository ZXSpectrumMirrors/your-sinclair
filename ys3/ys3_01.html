After being ignored for the best part of a year, a vague notion in the mind of Nathan Cross meets with the hyperactive enthusiasm of Jon Hyde. With only the scantest idea of what they're doing, the pair rapidly produce what is supposed to be the successor to the Great Magazine itself. On June 21st, 1999, the first issue of YS3 goes online.
