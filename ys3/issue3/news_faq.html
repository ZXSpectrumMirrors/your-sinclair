YS3 Makes It To The FAQ

After just two issues, the best Speccy magazine ever (well, the best ever based in Bristol. Apart from Spectacular.) has made it to the hallowed hall of fame that is the comp.sys.sinclair FAQ. What did the man who knows all things Sinclair, Philip Kendall, have to say about us?

'YS3 is hopefully going to be a montly on-line Speccy magazine; only one issue produced so far, which didn't have too much outstanding in it.'

Now is that enthusiasm or what? (Not really. - Jon) He said hopefully, didn't he? (And? - Jon) So he can't think we're that bad. Mind you, I can't agree with the statement about the first issue not having anything outstanding in it. What about that picture of Vanessa Angel, eh? (Ahem.)
