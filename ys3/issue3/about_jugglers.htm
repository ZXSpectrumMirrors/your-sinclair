Joystick Jugglers
    Nathan Cross
    This month Nathan's been a very busy bee. The ongoing saga of what operating system he uses is more or less over; Windows is no more. Nathan now uses Linux to do (almost) everything on his PC. Of course, he still has DOS: there's no Linux version of Martijn van der Heide's SGD yet, and he really likes the X128 emulator as well. Rumours of GTA, Doom and Quake addiction are entirely unproven.


    Jon Hyde
    Jon went AWOL for most of this month, promising reviews of a number of games and some truly inspirational features before completely vanishing from the face of the planet. He turned up one week before deadline dressed in a black suit, wearing black sunglasses, carrying a hefty zip-file of articles, a black briefcase that he never opened, and a strange scar on his left cheek. We thought it best not to ask what he'd been up to. Moving swiftly along...


    Jamie Percival
    After seeing The Phantom Menace, Jamie was inspired to become a Jedi Knight. He spent much of the month performing a series of physically-gruelling challenges at the behest of a small, green-skinned man with a voice not disimilar to that of Grover from Sesame Street. Unfortunately this person was not Jedi Master Yoda, but in fact a dwarf in camouflage make-up. Despite this, Jamie now calls himself 'Jam-Pe Sival' and has developed a rather disturbing habit of using The Force on bus conductors to avoid paying the fare.
