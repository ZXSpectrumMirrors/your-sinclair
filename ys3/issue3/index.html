    Hello Spec-chums!

    Isn't life wonderful? The sun is shining, the birds are singing, the sky is blue and the grass is green. On top of that, Issue Three of YS3 is out. Things can't get much better than this, can they?
    This month we've beefed up the content a bit with some proper news. And, of course, this month we've got a cover .tap, with an EXCLUSIVE, never-seen-before game that'll have you glued to the keyboard for days. Not only that, but this month's letters page actually contains some real letters! Er, that's not to say that previous pages didn't contain any. What is reality, anyway? All of our letters are real, on one level or another. Ahem. Anyway, enough banter. On with this month's issue.

    Cheers,
    Nathan
