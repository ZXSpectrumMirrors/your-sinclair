JOYSTICK JUGGLERS

Steven Harrison

Far away in the land of litigation, our cunningly-placed sleeper agent lurks amidst the unwitting citizens of the rebel colonies. Everybody thinks he's just a movie reviewer, giving advance warning of the dire films hurled at us from across the sea. But in reality, Agent Harrison is an agent of chaos, sowing the seeds of disharmoniosity wherever his feet may tread. You think this farce over the American election was down to badly-designed ballot papers? Think again, and be afraid. Be very afraid...
