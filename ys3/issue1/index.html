Issue One - June 21, 1999

OPI: 330

    Hello Spec-chums!

    Welcome to the first issue of a new on-line magazine dedicated to all things Spectrum. Updated monthly, it may not be as up-to-date as other sites, and it may not be as comprehensive on some issues. There may be access problems due to slow servers and poor connections. There are development problems thanks to lack of time and technical restrictions. And it seems a little pointless to review games that are more than a decade old. But (but! But!) it is reasonably entertaining, there are some nice pictures and things, and we can at least give you some idea of what's going on. I hope you enjoy what we've provided. If you have any suggestions, do e-mail us with them. (I like reading e-mail.)

    Nathan Cross
