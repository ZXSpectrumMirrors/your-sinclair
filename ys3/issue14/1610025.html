This was actually topical and funny when I wrote it. Unfortunately this issue is now so late that the character's actually left the show. God I'm annoying.

Number Fourteen - Lisa off Eastenders

Hello! My name's Nathan. I'm a writer for popular internet magazine YS3. Could I talk to you about trains?

Trains? What makes you think I know anything about trains?

Well, we're at a train station, and you're wearing an anorak.

So we're in a train station. So what? I'm just waiting for a train, that's all. And it... it looks like it might rain, so I'm wearing something waterproof. Is that alright with you?

Absolutely. So, what train are you waiting for?

Any train, I'm not fussy.

Aha!

Oh god, it's no good. I can't go on with the lying. You were right all along; I am a trainspotter. I've been telling everyone I've been going to that fancy Pierre in the High Street, or shopping up the West End, when in fact I've been sneaking off behind everyone's backs to catch the numbers off trains. (Sob.) But I can't help it! I've always loved trains! Why can't everyone just leave me alone to live my life?

I see you've brought your baby.

Yes... I know, it's wrong, I should've left her with somebody, but I couldn't bear to leave her with anyone. She might work out that just about anybody'd be a better mother than me, and then she'd never want to come back to me. Oh god, I'm so sorry...

So, er, how long have you been a trainspotter?

It's been going on for a while, from the moment I saw my first train. I know I should have told everybody sooner, but I just couldn't. I didn't want to hurt anybody, you've got to believe me! It's not my fault!

Oh, I'm sure it isn't. Erm... My, is that the time? Well, it's been nice talking to you. I must be going.

You're not going to tell anyone, are you? Please! Please don't tell anyone! Nathan!
