This conversation is based on a true story - the names have been omitted to protect the guilty.

You know, I'm not all that sure about evolution any more.

What do you mean? You're not turning to religion, are you?

Not really. Although now that you mention it, I have been considering Islam.

You've not been brainwashed by a passing terrorist, have you?

No, not at all. I was watching a documentary about Mohammed Ali the other day, and he seems to have done fairly well for himself.

Yes, but he went to prison.

What's that got to do with it? He was a conscientious objector.

Yes, but he was a black American in prison. He had to go Muslim.

That's rather a generalisation.

Look, everyone in prison turns to religion, don't they?

Well...

Alright, not everyone. But you get the idea.

Yes.

Now, what religion are most Americans?

Christian.

All the white folks are Christian. A black guy in America can't turn to Christianity - he'd have to lynch himself. So he turns away from the mainstream vanilla religions, and to the misunderstood religion of the oppressed.

Islam?

Exactly.

Hmm. You may have a point. Anyway. My point was that I don't believe in evolution any more.

So you're a creationist?

No.

But if you don't believe we evolved naturally, you must believe we were created.

Why?

Because it's one thing or the other.

Why?

It just is. It has to be one thing or the other. We occurred naturally or artificially. There is no third way.

Maybe there is, and we just don't know what it is.

That's just lunacy. You have to believe in something.

I do.

What?

I believe it's your round.

Damnation.
