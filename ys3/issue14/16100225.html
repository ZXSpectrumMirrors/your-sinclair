Loyalty

        There's not a lot worse than dating someone with all the moral fibre and ethical nature of a hyena. The more obsessively loyal and trustworthy you are, the more I'm going to like you. In fact, the more obsessive about me you are, the more I'm going to like you. (I'm easily pleased.) If you have the fickle nature of, well, practically everyone I've dated this year, then don't even bother replying. Please.

Logic

        It's inevitable that we will, at some point, disagree. Disagreements can happen because of two reasons - bad data or bad logic. Disagreements of the former kind are easily corrected - whoever's right provides the other with good data. Disagreement over. If you suffer from consistently bad logic, I'm going to get annoyed very quickly. (And based on previous experience, probably continue dating you for two and a half years.)
