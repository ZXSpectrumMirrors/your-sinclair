A bit of a contracted Jugglers this issue, since the number of contributors amounts to, well, just me. That's right, this issue was written by one person. No, I didn't think it was possible, either. So, er, I suppose I'd better get on with writing my entry, hadn't I?

Right then. I've not really done all that much since the last issue. Erm... I did some work on a building site. I gave lots of people bad news about their holiday plans. I even helped a few people with their insurance claims. What else? Um... My psychic abilities improved. I'm now single. I've finished Halo. Crikey, this writing-about-yourself lark's a bit tricky. I'll tell you what, let me off this time and I'll write an essay on 'What I Did In My Summer Holiday' for the next ish. How does that sound? Good-oh.
