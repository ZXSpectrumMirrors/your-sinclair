Number Twenty-Nine - Osama Bin Laden

Once an infamous villain that stalked the moonlit streets of America, Osama's evil plans could be counted on to be thwarted by Batman and the rest of the Justice League. It's now well-known, of course, that he was at one point captured, but rather than be put into Arkham Asylum to rot alongside the likes of the Joker, the Riddler and Mr Freeze, Osama Bin Laden (or The Beard, as he prefers to be known) was offered a deal by SINISTER GOVERNMENT AGENTS. Osama regained his freedom, and in return The Beard went on to single-handedly smash the Red Army, succeeding where Superman, Rocky, Hitler and Bugs Bunny had failed.

Things looked bad for the SINISTER GOVERNMENT AGENTS, who had never really expected Osama to survive. But survive he did, and turned down the offer of a renewal of his contract (which would have committed him to invading Moscow in November and leading an English cricket team to victory), opting instead for the excitement of independent freedom-fighting/terrorism (delete depending on whose side you're on). Gathering up the names and deeds of countries responsible for the worst atrocities against mankind, the one with the most black marks against it turned out to be, ironically, the land of superheroes, SINISTER GOVERNMENT AGENTS and (you knew it was coming) Mom's apple pie - the United States of America.

Realising that even The Beard could not defeat the Justice League alone, Osama decided to form his own band of freedom fighters. Naming it the Foundation for Law And Government, or FLAG, Osama was quickly threatened with legal action by the producers of Knight Rider, and shortened the name to simply 'The Foundation'. Its numbers grew very slowly at first, and even slower when Osama revealed to his family that some shooting may be involved. In desperation, Osama hit on the idea of a telethon. Appealing to the oppressed masses of the world, he quickly gathered idealistic followers of all ages and backgrounds. Many even went so far as to grow beards to further emulate their new hero.
