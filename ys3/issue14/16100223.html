2. Plan elaborate in-depth features.

Such as, for example, a series on how to program a Speccy in assembly language. Plan seemingly straightforward features that are in reality just big holes that will consume days or even weeks of your free time, and you're guaranteed to make issues late and annoy your readers. Even better, plan and promise huge features, and then completely fail to deliver them for several issues running. That'll wind everyone up.
