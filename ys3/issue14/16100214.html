Hello! Nathan here again. As you may or may not be aware, I'm currently single. This isn't really much of a problem, apart from the soul-destroying loneliness, but I'm told that it would be in my best interests to find a new girlfriend. For reasons that I won't be going into right now, I'm not going to look for one. Instead, I'm going to wait for a potential mate to find me.

Naturally, I'm desperate enough that I'll consider anybody with the right gender on their birth certificate, but eventually a few other issues are going to be raised. With that in mind, here's a quick run-down on what I'm looking for...

Humour

        You really need to have a sense of humour. I'm not looking for a comedienne or anything, and I'd probably get a bit paranoid if you were funnier than me. Ideally you find YS3 funny in the right places, and you'll be able to tell me when something I've written is funny or not, thus saving me from writing an awful lot of useless material. (It's too late to save this issue, though. Jon)

Interests

        I don't have all that many serious hobbies. I'm into computers, as should be obvious by the fact that I'm not only editing a web-based magazine but trying to use it to attract women. I'm not too concerned about what hobbies a potential girlfriend has, just so long as she has at least one. (If you're into writing random yet entertaining essays of about 250-500 words length, that'd be great.) If your only interests are hair, make-up, clothes and pop music, I'm probably going to get bored after about twenty minutes.
