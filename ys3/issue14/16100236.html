    Due to a hilarious misunderstanding, our volunteers remained in the test chamber for another twelve hours. In spite of our error, we were nevertheless pleased that the data gleaned from this extended experiment would be all the more valid for our mistake. It was with this in mind that we released our volunteers for the final time and asked them their thoughts on the study.

I'm in the process of applying for a DTI grant to further investigate the effects of rap music, and I expect to turn in a more satisfactory report once the money runs out. I have been Professor Winston K. Pomegranate-Hugely, and I bid you good day.
