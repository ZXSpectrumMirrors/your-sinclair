    The next step in our highly scientific study was to obtain volunteers willing to be subjected to intensive and prolonged exposure to rap music. Traditionally a fair sum of money is used to offset the potential danger volunteers expose themselves to. In this particular study, however, we were lucky enough to be approached by two volunteers who refused to be paid. Without further ado, we locked them in a darkened room and began playing the twenty-track, seventy-seven minutes album by one Marshall Mathers - The Eminem Show.
