JOYSTICK JUGGLERS

Nat Cross

The more vociferous of the dynamic duo responsible for YS3's creation, Nathan 'Nat' Cross is probably most well-known for his unreliability when it comes to issue releases. In his defence, there aren't very many people around who'd be happy to sideline their lives in order to round up a dozen articles from a group of people often only slightly more reliable than him, check and correct the spelling and grammar, arrange everything in the proper format and publish it online very often, let alone fourteen times, and certainly not while writing their own articles as well, so the only complaints he's accepting these days are those from people who've actually edited a magazine.

Not only has Nathan edited YS3 from day one, but he's also written vast amounts of material for it, and been responsible for all of the artwork. (Apart from the decent-looking logos.)
