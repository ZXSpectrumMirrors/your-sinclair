Assembly Studio Options for Bintap

Click Tools, then Options. In the External Tools tab, click Add. The options in my copy are set as follows:

Display Name: Bintap

Command: (path to Bintap)Bintap.exe

Arguments: $ProgPath.obj $ProgName.tap $ProgName

Initial Directory: (Blank)
