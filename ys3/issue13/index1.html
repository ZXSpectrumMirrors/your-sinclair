Hello readers!

That's right - we're back. No, really this time. It's taken long enough, I know; it's six months since the Christmas 2000 issue, and the year's almost over. Still, you get what you pay for, as they say, and given that nobody's sent us any money this issue shouldn't really be here. Still, it's written now, so you might as well read it, I suppose.

Do let us know what you think of this issue; any suggestions or comments can be addressed, as usual, to letters@ys3.co.uk. The next issue will be out closer to on time than this one, so if there's any Spectrum-related news you have, do your bit for the community and send that to us too.

Anyway. There's plenty of magazine here for you to enjoy, so I'll let you get on with it.

Cheers,
Nathan Cross, editor
