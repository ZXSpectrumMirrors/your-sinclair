Friday

Jamie took the helm today, deciding that enough was enough. Jon was happily delivering his papers when the vengeful YS3 journalist’s request from his SG-C mates in the air force came through. An entire squadron of F-14s came tearing over the small hamlet in which Jon operated. Shadows of fighter planes came streaking across the ground, followed by the hail of missiles that were their combined payload. Jon was not unprepared, having borrowed Jamie’s personal Gou’ald forcefield, and so the explosions failed to touch him and his bike. The houses of the hamlet were not so fortunate, and smoking rubble was almost all that remained. Almost. In a remarkably unlikely move of Fate, the one subscriber who was left on Jon’s list remained untouched. Phew.
