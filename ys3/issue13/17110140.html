Back in the mists of time when the Great Magazine was at its celebrated silliest, there appeared on the scene five not so intrepid heroes, bearing an uncanny resemblance to some of the most famous children’s characters ever. Reviewing one game, Sleepwalker, they were subsequently cast adrift into the Speccy wilderness. YS3 caught up with the Fumous Five on their latest adventure: a visit to a farm in Devon.

Dink: Gosh, look at all those cows, chaps. Just think, one day they’ll be beef. Mmm... we’ll eat them with Yorkshire Pudding and horseradish sauce.

(grins greedily and licks his lips)

Julio: You won’t be eating these cows, Dink. They’re off to get slaughtered and burned next week.

Flan: Oh, the poor creatures! What have they done to deserve that?

Tummy: Woof!

Gorge: Yes, I agree, Tummy. Rabbit is much nicer!

Julio: Not as tasty as you though, eh? (leers)

Gorge: Shut up. Is that really the best you can come up with?

Flan: Oh, do be quiet, you two. Julio, why are these cows going to be destroyed?

Julio: They’re diseased, Flan. It’s spreading fast.

Dink: We must do something to stop it, Julio. This sounds like a job for the Fumous Five!

Gorge: Yeah!

Tummy: Woof!
