Paint The Whole World With A Rainbow

Sir,

I claim a Trainspotters Award, for noticing a glaring inaccuracy in your magazine.

I have just got back from holiday, and had rather a lot of mail to go through.

Anyway, on page 12 Issue 3 March 1986 (Hang on a minute... Ed)there is a grossly misleading advert! I can only conclude that it was an April Fools joke gone wrong. A company supposedly called Micronet800 are advertising - a modem based service including FREE games, FREE e-mail and home shopping. For twenty pence per day. But, the telephone number was clearly wrong 'cos it started 01 not 0171! (Well, that's because... Ed)

Undeterred I went to London, clutching my 20p, to the published address (8 Herbal Hill LONDON EC1), only to find a juggling shop! There was a little man inside the shop smoking a 'herbal' cigarette, I told him that I had come about the Spectrum. He took his sunglasses off, laughed manically and said 'Yeah Man, paint the whole world with a rainbow'.

So, I deserve an award, or at least compensation for my wasted journey.
