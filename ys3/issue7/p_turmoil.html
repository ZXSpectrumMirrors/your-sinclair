YS3 Megapreview!
Chaos is without doubt one of the best multiplayer strategy games ever. Games such as Command and Conquer pale beside Julian Gollop's classic masterpiece. Luckily for us, Dan Condon-Jones agrees with us. And, luckier still, Mr Condon-Jones is a rather good programmer.

Currently being developed for Windows, Turmoil - The Battle of Wizards is a remake of that venerable classic that brings all of the tension and drama of those rubber-keyed days to the PC. At the time of writing much of the main game engine looks to be in place, along with a lot of very flashy special effects. It plays very similarly to the original, albeit slightly easier to control thanks to the mouse interface - no more trying to remember what the keys are!

As you can see from the screenshots, it looks amazing, and it looks like it'll have the gameplay to match. Check out Dan's site for more details.
