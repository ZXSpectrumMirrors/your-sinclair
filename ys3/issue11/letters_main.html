Where have all the slices gone?

Dear YS3,

I recently bought a box of Mr Kipling's Toffee Cream Slices after they were blatantly plugged in your magazine (I would have bought some earlier, but apparently Mr Kipling had to personally drive them down to the YS3 offices and none of the rest of the country actually got any). Gorgeous as they are, I can't figure out how to eat them without the filling squirting out and ending up in the carpet. I wonder if your magazine would be so kind as to do a feature on how to eat the beasts?

Chris Young, via e-mail

Looks like you’re a victim of Pseudo-Toffee Cream Slices. They look like the real thing, and taste quite a bit like the real thing, but the difference is in the workmanship. So poor is it that the cream flies out of the edges when pressure is applied, rather then masterfully staying inside despite all the laws of physics as is the case for the real thing. Take them back and call the police, is our advice. You’re lucky to still be alive.
