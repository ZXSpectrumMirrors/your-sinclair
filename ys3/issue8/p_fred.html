YS3 Megapreview!
Two games in one issue - only John Dow would dare to do such a thing. A few quick calculations report that Mr Dow is currently working on forty- three remakes at the moment, and I'm really looking forward to this one.

Fred is an archaeologist in the mould of Indiana Jones. Trapped in an ancient pyramid, he has to face ghosts, ghouls, spiders and snakes ('I hate snakes') in his quest to nobble as much of the pharoah's treasure as possible and escape.

We've had a chance to play an early version, and it's shaping up pretty nicely. The graphics, as you can see, are recoloured from the originals, and the control routines are much like the original. We'll have a review for you at Mr Dow's earliest convenience (or when he finishes the game, whichever comes first).
