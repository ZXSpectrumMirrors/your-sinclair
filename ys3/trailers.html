ISSUE TRAILERS

It's a well-known fact that most issues of YS3 are late. Sometimes, though, issues are so late that rumours start springing up that Nathan's finally moved to Cuba, that Jon's started miming typing his articles, and YS3 is no more. It's after these long absences that Nathan likes to quell rumours of disappearance by putting out a contract on the alarmists' heads. Unfortunately, that would be illegal (apparently), so instead he writes exceedingly long (and occasionally even readable) posts to comp.sys.sinclair as a hilariously inept attempt at marketing the magazine to the apathetic.

Here, at last, are the 'special editions' of those wonderful trailers.
