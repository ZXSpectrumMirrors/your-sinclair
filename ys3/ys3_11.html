Issue Eleven - November 2000
[Browse][Download]


This month sees a surprising turn of events, as Nathan's hospitalisation leads to Jon being forced to fill his shoes and edit an issue of YS3. Although a tight fit (because Nathan's shoes are several sizes too small for him), Jon pushes on and successfully completes one of the better issues of the magazine, mainly because he knows it could be his only chance to answer the letters.
    This month also sees the removal of the increasingly lengthy (and dull to write) Back Issues page, with the creation of The YS3 Rock'n'Roll Years. Thanks to Mr Nick Humphries (a man who probably gets more credit on this site than on his own, or indeed the people who wrote the issues of YS3), all of the loveliness that is YS3 is now online, from the very first issue to whichever the last one Nathan's got around to cleaning up, turning into a zip file and sending to him.
