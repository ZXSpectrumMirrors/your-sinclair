Obviously there's always the possibility of legal capers when it comes to this sort of thing. With this in mind, the YS3 team have endeavoured, where possible, to avoid references to the Great Magazine. You'll note that there is nothing directly stolen, apart from the general layout and style. The worst form of copyright breach was in the Outrun Europa feature, which included a scanned page of the review in order to question the truthfulness of a reviewer. We may also have libelled Future Publishing, for a laugh.
