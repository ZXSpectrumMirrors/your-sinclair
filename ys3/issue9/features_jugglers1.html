The Jugglers On
Jon Hyde

1. Why did you start YS3?

I’ve always dreamed of writing for a computer mag ever since I bought Total and Super Play from Future Publishing, so when Nathan came along with the offer of writing for YS3, it was an offer I couldn’t refuse. Especially with the knowledge that we were following in the footsteps of the orig – sorry? We can’t use that word here? Oh, sorry. Guess that’s that then.

2. What inspires you to write something every month (or thereabouts)?

The knowledge that all those readers out there really want me to. That, and I get this odd twitch if I don’t review or criticise something for more than a fortnight.

3. What's your favourite Speccy game?

One-player, it would have to be Rainbow Islands. If that game was a woman, I’d have swept her off her feet and be happily married in Eastbourne by now. The two-player award goes to Chaos. Never has setting a dragon on your best friend been so much fun, and there’s the whole epistemic dimension of it’s a dragon... or is it?!

4. Favourite snack?

Twix. Or if I’m on a sugar-overload, salt and vinegar crisps.

5. Favourite TV show?

Due South. It rocks. What other show has the immortal line. “This is Chicago, so unless someone shoots a moose this is out of your jurisdiction!”

6. Favourite film?

The Empire Strikes Back. It has it all: violence, spaceships, lightsabres, revelations, philosophy… and who could forget the moment when they realise it isn’t an cave they’ve landed in… and Yoda rocks. I just love the way his ears change when he replies to Luke’s claim that he’s not frightened with “oh, you will be!”. I had it on video when I was younger and watched it so many times I got sick of it, but then I waited ten years before seeing it again and I’d forgotten all that happened. Heck, I’d forgotten it was part of the Star Wars trilogy and was about to write to George Lucas and tell him there were only two films in his so-called “trilogy”. Classic stuff.

7. How many kiwi birds does it take to change a light bulb?

Well, I have a light bulb here, and a box of Kiwi Birds... maybe I should try one… they’re so cute and crispy... I... (gobble, crunch, squeak. Burp.) Sorry. Got another box?
