The Lion King, despite being a Disney movie, is actually one of the cinematic greats. It has the epic style of Star Wars, it has the tragic elements of Shakespeare, and it also has some really great songs. And if that wasn't enough, it's also got some perfectly timed comedy. At no point while watching do you feel silly for watching a kid's film. The Lion King appeals to all ages, and entertains all. A work of true genius.
