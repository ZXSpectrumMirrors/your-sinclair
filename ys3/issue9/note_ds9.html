Despite the fact that Babylon 5 had been in production in one form or another since the mid-eighties, DS9 devotees insisted that B5 was a rip- off of their beloved show. They then proceeded to deny that DS9 was ripping off B5 in any way when it introduced what passes for an ongoing plot with Paramount's scriptwriters, and started claiming that there had always been a plan for what the ongoing story would be. This is, of course, a complete lie.

B5 fans are known to try, on occasion, convincing these poor deluded fools of their misguided nature, and persuading them to watch the best show television ever produced. DS9 fans almost always decline, claiming such things as 'the B5 universe is really backward, because they don't have shields or transporters'. The truth is that DS9 fans are generally small children, or are otherwise incapable of dealing with realism on television.
