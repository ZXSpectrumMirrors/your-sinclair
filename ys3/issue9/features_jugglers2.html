The Jugglers On
Jamie Percival

1. Why did you join YS3?

I used to read the original YS and dream about being a software reviewer... playing games and getting paid for it... what a life. I used to run a similar site a few years ago, but I removed it because it was to say the least.... crap.

2. What inspires you to write something every month (or thereabouts)?

Er... every month? Ahem... Dunno really, I just do... one of those subliminal things I guess. Oh, and when I have some time spare... which isn't very often.

3. What's your favourite Speccy game?

Hard one. I liked lots. I guess it's between Strontium Dog: the Killing and Sabre Wulf. I like mazey type games.

4. Favourite snack?

Hmmm... crisps. Bacon or Cheese 'n' onion.

5. Favourite TV show?

The Simpsons or Futurama. Don't watch much telly.

6. Favourite film?

I can see I'm going to be laughed off the face of the earth, but without doubt, it's The Lion King. I'll get me coat...
