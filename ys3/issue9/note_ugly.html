Clint Eastwood is a damned fine actor, and his westerns are generally perceived as being some of the best there are. The Good, The Bad and The Ugly is a typical classic. If you haven't seen it, go out and get hold of the video, along with A Fistful of Dollars and For A Few Dollars More, both by the same director (Sergio Leone) and featuring the same principal character (The Man With No Name).
