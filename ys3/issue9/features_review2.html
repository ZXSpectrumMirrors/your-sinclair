One month on, and these upstarts show no signs of going away. In fact, there are more of them, thanks to Jamie Percival. A mildly improved selection of articles, this time, with those tedious lists consigned to the bin. And thanks to Gerard Sweeney's input, the girlie this month is Lisa from Steps. By this time people had started reading, and the first Trainspotter Award was claimed, but not awarded...
