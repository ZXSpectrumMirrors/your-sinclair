cover tape
Cover tape: Rebelstar (Mythos Games, unreviewed), Soldier One (Beyond Belief, unreleased), The Pathetic Pablo Bros (Steve Anderson, YS), Ultra-208 +3 utility (Digital Reality), DT+ +D utility (Rumatisoft), NMI2 music demo (Dynamite Dynastie), and the usual POKEs proggy.
    Craig Broadbent gets a little sick of only puzzle games being released for the SAM, and his review of puzzle compilation Dyadic gets a bit of a backlash from those in the SAM publishing comunity...
    Another first for YS - no previews whatsoever - the end of the Speccy games scene is nigh...
