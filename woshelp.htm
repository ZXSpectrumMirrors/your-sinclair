Hopefully this page will now have split into two, and the bottom window will have the World Of Spectrum (WOS) search results.
    Scroll down the bottom window and look for any hyperlinks containing "Z80", "SNA", "TZX", "TAP", "DSK", "SLT", plus any possible new extensions which might look like a Spectrum game snapshot. Bear in mind your emulator might not support all those formats - look in the documentation for your emulator to find out what formats it supports.
    I'm often asked how to play these Spectrum games once you've downloaded them. Well, first you'll need a Spectrum emulator for your machine. There are well over 100 emulators available for a multitude of platforms that I won't even attempt to list them all here. Instead, go to the comp.sys.sinclair FAQ Emulators page where you're bound to find something for your machine and operating system.
    After downloading and installing the emulator, read the instructions - I can't help you out with any questions about how to use an emulator.

After reading your emulator documentation and downloading your desired Spectrum game, you have all you need to go into retro heaven!
