We Want Your Screens!

You can get a list of the most wanted screenshots here.

If you fancy doing something fun and also something that'll help The Your Sinclair Rock'n'Roll Years, here's the perfect solution: play loads of Speccy games!
     The YSRnRY needs your help. We need screenshots. Lot's of 'em. We've got screens for the vast majority of the games mentioned in the first thirty issues of YS, but that's not enough - we're aiming for as close to total coverage as possible.
    So, send us any screenshots you have made, or, if you're feeling particularly generous, pick an issue which has missing screenshots and fill in the gaps. Owners of Speccy game CDs like the WOS CD will find this much easier as they won't have to download some games before they play them. It'll be a good idea to send me a quick email before you do an issue's worth to verify that that issue hasn't been done yet. Here's a list of the most-wanted screenshots.
    Any picture format is welcome, so long as it isn't in a lossy format (so GIF, PCX, BMP and PNG is OK, but JPEG isn't). It doesn't matter if the shot includes the border or not (i.e. x128 screenshots includes a bit of the BORDER, Z80 doesn't) - I'll chop out the border later.
    One important thing to remember is that we're after in-game screenshots, NOT loading screens, nor front-ends.
    Send your screenshots to screens@ysrnry.co.uk
    Extra screenshots should appear on the site within the two weeks after you've sent them to me.
    Many thanks for any help you can give me.
