Your Computer

February 1988
Issue 26

Free badge on the cover - yes, even you could have owned a "Wagga Wagga" badge...
    Duncan McDonald joins YS, and the world is a wierder place for it...
    YS Critic's Games of the Year 1987 announced. First came Head Over Heels (Ocean), second The Sentinel (Firebird) and third Mercenary (Novagen).
    Role Playing Games feature.
    Operation Wolf, Afterburner and Xenophobe are featured in Slots of Fun.
    More US Gold arcade conversions are revealed with the announcement of a major deal between them and Capcom, traditionally associated with Elite.
    John Minson makes some light-hearted predictions for 1988, including plans for a Sunday Sport Simulator - a joystick waggler, naturally...
