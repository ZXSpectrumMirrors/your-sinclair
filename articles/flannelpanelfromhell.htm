    [Apparently Jonathan left off Kevin Cox, and was chased around the office for that... --NickH]
    
Well, that's it then. The end of YS. The Shed looks so bare now everything's packed up in crates. (So we'll crate that up as well.) Traditionally the Ed gets all blubby and reflective on the last page and thanks loads of people in a Dickie Attenborough fashion, but is that the YS way? Crikey, no! So get yourself a small plastic bag, fill it with water, twist the top closed, tighten and knot it. Now run outside and hurl it at the nearest body of people, shouting "This one's for Spec-chums everywhere!" Hurrah! (You're under arrest. A policeman) Lawks, what a surprise ending. (The Ed is dragged away.) Remember folks! (Struggle.) Be crap to each other! In a funky skillo sort of way!
