SAM Juggler Demo (SAM)
Codigo £Free May 1992 YS77
80
Jon Pillar
Surpassing the ESI Surprise Demo in no uncertain terms, this excellent machine-code program shows off the Coupe's graphics to fine effect. A series of neatly-drawn pictures introduce the programming crew and their country of origin, before the demo launches into an amazing ray-traced animation of, well, SAM juggling. It's eminently watchable, mildly hypnotic and in short the kind of demo that should be playing on Coupe screens everywhere.
