Sean Kelly

Captain Blood/Exxos (YS41, 68°)
   Curious game, proving difficult to get to grips with, but which might benefit from perseverence.
Double Dragon II/Melbourne House (YS49, 77°)
   A snazzy conversion of a fairly average coin-op. Great fun at first, but probably not all that long-lasting.
Eliminator/Hewson (YS41, 90°)
   Hewson maintains its high standards with this rootin', tootin', killin', maimin', shoot 'em up. Not to be missed by arcadey types.
Exploding Fist +/Firebird (YS38, 7/10)
   Rehash of the original with two opponents to fight this time, but ultimately not worthy of its predecessor.
H.A.T.E./Gremlin (YS42, 90°)
   Mega shoot 'em up from Gremlin. Nothing incredibly imaginative, but what's there is excellently programmed and brilliantly executed.
Heatwave (Compilation)/Hewson (YS44, 90°)
   Six of the best from Hewson. Fun-packed variety and unbeatable value.
Professional BMX Simulator/Codemasters Plus (YS35, 8/10)
   Excellent value for money simulation from Code Masters, well presented with fabby tunes.
Rex/Martech (YS36, 9/10)
   This has to be the best shoot'em up since Cybernoid. I love it to death.
Thunder Blade/US Gold (YS38, 9/10)
   Brilliant scrolling and graphics combine to make an excellent shoot 'em up and faithful conversion from the coin-op.
