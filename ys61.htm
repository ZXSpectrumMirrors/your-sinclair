cover tape
Another good tape: Quazatron (Hewson, 9, June 1986), Chubby Gristle (Grandslam, 5, September 1988), Hydrofool (Gargoyle, 8, August 1987 & Rack-it, 8/10, January 1989) and demos of Gazza II (Empire) and The Hunt For Red October (Grandslam).
    Free badge on the cover.
    Joysticks feature.
    Another look at Speccy-clone the Hobbit.
    Storm announces a third Jetman game, some six or so years after the last one. Entitled Solar Jetman In The Quest For The Golden Warship, it didn't eventually come out for the Speccy.
    US Gold gets the Super Monaco Grand Prix arcade licence.
    Meanwhile, Ocean picks up the film licence of the year: Terminator 2.
