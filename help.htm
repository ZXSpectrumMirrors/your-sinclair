Issue Summary Tables

These are the pages which give summaries of what's in each issue, complete with ratings.
    Did you know that the thumbnail of the magazine cover is clickable? If you do that, you'll download the full-size scan of the cover.
    The summary page itself is split into the following sections:



    The Articles section is a list of all the articles that I've scanned from this issue and put onto this site. Game names with (SAM) after it means that it's a review for a game for the SAM Coupe.
    The General section gives a brief overview of the features in that issue of the magazine, along with details of what's on the cover tape, and what news was reported for that month.
    Clicking the tape icon goes to the cover tape section of the World of Spectrum Archive so you can download the contents of that month's cover tape.
    The Previews and Review sections are game previews and reviews. The Misc Reviews section lists reviews for Adventure games, utilities and other stuff.

You'll find the following icons next to game names, and they do the following actions relating to the game they're next to:

Search WoS Archive
View Game Screenshot
Read Original Review
Read Re-Release Review
Read Entry in Compilation Review

Reviews

A typical review has a header similar to this one:

Firefly
Special FX £7.95/14.95 Apr 1988 YS28





 
A simply super shoot 'em up/strategy game that keeps you coming back for more. Don't be a dork - buy it!

    The contents of the table are fairly self-explanitory. You can do two things in this table. Clicking on the issue details takes you to the summary page for that issue. Clicking on the WOS button searches the World Of Spectrum archive for the game being reviewed, in this case Firefly.
