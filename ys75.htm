cover tape
Cover tape: Firelord (Hewson, 7, Dec'86), Impossaball (Hewson, 9, Mar'87 & Players, 85%, Feb'91), Pixy the Microdot II (David Box, YS), Professional Speedboat Simulator demo (Mark Thompson), Devastating Blow demo (Beyond Belief), STK toolkit (Osvald Pinali Doederlein, YS) and POKEs program.
    New section "The World" is started and covers film, music, video and book news and reviews.
    Article on game sequels.
    "Haylp!" problem page launched. Later described by Jon Pillar as "the worst thing ever in YS".
    In response to large software companies leaving the Spectrum games scene, Support Our Speccy is launched. Readers are invited to send letters to software houses to plead for more software.
