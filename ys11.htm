November 1986
Issue 11

Amstrad release ZX Spectrum+2 with built-in tape deck, previewed in YS.
    Romantic Robot's Multiface One released.
    PSS, not having learned their lesson the first time, start advertising the never-to-be-seen Swords & Sorcery 2 (Heroquest) - no relation to the later Hero Quest from Games Workshop/Gremlin.
    Many thanks to Jim Grimwood for supplying this entry!
    Max Phillips notices the rise in popularity of computer geeks, warning that they're about to have the last laugh.
