Way back in 1997, I put together the Your Sinclair Rock'n'Roll Years. The idea was a simple one, inspired by the BBC's Rock'n'Roll Years TV series. The BBC version had movie clips of news reports of events of a given year, subtitles that gave some background information about what was going on, and was mixed with a music soundtrack made up of songs from that year.

The Your Sinclair Rock'n'Roll Years took that idea and put it into webpage form for each issue of YS. Each page had links to news and reviews for each relevant issue.

It was rather nifty. Still is, in fact. Perfect for anyone with a modem who longs for a quick shot of Speccy nostalgia.

And now broadband has arrived, and it's time for something a bit beefier. It's time to come full circle and do what the BBC originally did.

It's time for The Your Sinclair Rock'n'Roll Years TV show!
