A few people have commented on the 3D animations that appear within the documentary chapters, so I thought some desktop wallpapers of high-detail rendered frames from each of the scenes might go down well.

I've created wallpapers for regular and widescreen sized desktops at 1600x1200 and 1920x1200 respectively - feel free to scale and crop to your own taste. And I highly recommend downloading the PNG files - the extra clarity makes the image stunning when viewed full-screen, especially on my 24" widescreen LCD!
