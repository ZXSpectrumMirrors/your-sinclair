21st December 2006 : Merry Christmas! After a slightly longer development period than I had anticipated, the 1987 chapter is now available for download!
    It's 7m37s long, 76MB in size, and you can download using HTTP, BitTorrent or just stream it from Google Video.
    See the new threat to the Spectrum! Wince at Amstrad's poor response to it! Cheer as the best Spectrum game ever made is released! Scream as a certain tune refuses to stop playing inside your head! Groan as US Gold botches OutRun! And applaud Sir Clive as he follows his own path in life!
    Folks, this is the sixth chapter in the series, and marks the half-way point. The total documentary duration is now 46m24s. Considering that I was originally concerned that I wouldn't be able to fill a half-hour, the projected hour and a half total is looking *really* good right now. You cannot imagine how much fun I'm having doing these.
    Enjoy this early Christmas present, and hopefully next year I'll manage to get 1988, 1989 and 1990 chapters out in 2007.
