A selection of YS Memorabilia that didn't seem to fit in anywhere else in this website.

YS badge letter.

Matthew Wilson has sent in the letter that accompanied the YS badge he received for getting a letter published during Jonathan Nash's reign as editor. The letter reads:

Dear YS Reader,

    Thank you very much for your letter/comment/contribution/badger/whatever. It gave us hours of enjoyment/pleasure/slight discomfort/waiting irritably in line for a tetanus injection/lectures/guitar solos/detailed instructions on how to rob the Royal Mint.
    Find enclosed an almost legendary YS badge to make you feel all warm inside.

Happy trails,

Jonathan

Your Sinclair Apology Letter on behalf of Romantic Robot.

This letter was sent out to everyone that ordered a Multiface 128 using the special form printed in YS (Aug 87 'Gobots Cover' page 27) which offered the device for a dirtcheap £24.95!! Unfortunately, the price was incorrect and everyone that took the offer was asked to stump up the extra £15.
I decided to cancel the order as I didn't have enough money, but Romantic Robot still sent me a Multiface 128 for only £24.95 anyway!! I'm a jammy git! Thanks Romantic Robot!!
As to the amount of people that cancelled, I don't know, but if half of them did, then it would've made a massive dent in their profit margin.

I took this letter to one of the ZX Microfairs and asked all the YS staff that attended the show to sign the letter :o)
Dr Berkman, Mad Monk, ZZKJ, Carol, T'Zer, Peter Shaw, Tony Lee.
T'zer's reaction... "Oh Shit..."

Actual size: A4 Sheet of paper
