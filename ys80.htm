For the first time in YS history, YS has a non-Spectrum subject on it's cover advertising its Batman Returns movie review.
    Linda Barker becomes editor.
    Cover tape: Defenders of the Earth (Enigma Variations, 64%, June'90 & Hi-Tec, 64% May'91), Forbidden Planet (Design Design, 7, Mar'86), Conman the Barbaric demo (The Northern Underground), Logo (Mat and Ziutek, YS), E-Sampler utility (Proxima, YS), Beruska PD demo (Marek Fiser), plus POKEs.
    YS returns to Alton Towers.
    Sly Spy Secret Agent resurfaces after being pulled at the last moment when it was originally released.
