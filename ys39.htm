cover tape
Cover tape has Army Moves (Imagine, 8, July 1987) and a demo of its sequel, Navy Moves.
    YS Photo Love Story "Love in a Cold Climate".
    Valentines Day Vox Pop.
    Clive Sinclair (48) announces engagement to Bernadette Tynan (22).
    After the disappointment to many Speccy users of the PC 200, YS finally gets around to covering another computer in the making - MGT's SAM Coupe. Planned specs were: Z80B microprocessor, 256k RAM (upgradeable to 512k), four graphics modes, including one Spectrum-like mode and one hi-res 512x192 mode with 64 colours, six-channel sound and built-in disk drive capability. Planned retail price was £150 and the Coupe was going to be the first in a line of SAM products. Computer will be ready "in May".
    Chartbusting bestseller Robocop reviewed and underrated.
    US Gold throws the Christmas gauntlet by announcing one of their big licences for the end of '89: Ghouls and Ghosts.
