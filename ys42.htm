The Games Machine


ACE

June 1989
Issue 42

 
View/download
cover tape
Very very crap cover tape - Explorer (Electric Dreams, unreviewed in YS, but other mags gave it around 25%) and a demo of System 3's Dominator. A sign of things to come?
    Shock news - T'zer gets promoted, so Matt Bielby gets bumped up to the Editor's post.
    Lightguns appear on the Speccy, and prove to be just a fad.
    Second-hand Speccy buyers guide: 48k £40, 48k+ £50, 128k £80, +2 £100, +3 £150.
    St Dragon is covered in Slots of Fun.
    Activision announces it's big licences for the year, the highlights being Power Drift, Altered Beast, Dynamite Dux and Ghostbusters II.
    YS starts to advertise on the radio.
    National Rescue makes it's last appearance.
