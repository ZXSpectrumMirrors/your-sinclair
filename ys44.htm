The Games Machine


ACE

August 1989
Issue 44

 
View/download
cover tape
Crap, but unique cover tape, because not only does it have Skateboard Construction System (Players, 2, December 1988) and a demo of Tynesoft's Buffalo Bill's Rodeo Games, it also has David "Whistling Rick" Wilson singing Hold My Hand Very Tightly!
    MGT starts advertising its SAM Coupe hotline.
    YS Personality Test.
    Strider and Golden Axe appear in Slots of Fun.
    Elite announces that it will end the full-price side of its operations after the release of the next few games. Meanwhile, Elite announces that all the games on its budget label Encore will be sold for £1.99. Apart from those that are sold at £2.99.
    A colourful game by the name of Crazy Space Racer got bought by Virgin, who had the bright idea of releasing it as Dan Dare III.
    Sir Clive Sinclair splits up with Bernadette Tynan.
