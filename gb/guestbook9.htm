This site is crap! (in a kinda funky skillo sort of way!)
Ahh those were the days! Everytime I look at a spectrum magazine or see a website it brings me back to my school days where I would often call up Jonathan Pillar/Nash and have a chat about whatever! It was great fun, and he was a great guy. He even sent me a free magazine! I remember the cassette was really clear and had a speedloader program on it hehe it was great!
There was also this time when i called up and spoke to Linda Barker, she thought it was a friend of hers when I told her to 'Stop barking up the wrong Pillar!' oh how we laughed!
I remember one time talking to Jonathan and asking to reprint the scroll-a-load program in Program Pitstop. There was another time when I suggested placing parts of the magazine onto the covertape, things they had to leave out since the magazine was barely larger than the leaflet i get from my local store. Well look what appears on the following months covertape, yep! the very first edition of YS2!
Sadly no matter how much I chatted to the gang over the phone, I never got my name mentioned in the magazine *cries*.
The other thing was seeing the first picture of the work experience babe, Rebecca Norley!! Since then, until now I havent stopped dreaming of her.. that was like 10yrs or so ago now? I know im sad (probably).
Keep up the good work! What would be great is if we managed to get some of the former YS people to write new material. Maybe produce a special anniversary edition of YS when an appropriate time comes. What do you think?

I went back on the Java Spectrum Emulator, and tried the old Teleporter trick on their version of Manic Miner - it works!

During play, type in "6031769" (minus the quote marks), and a boot will appear next to your remaining lives. Now hold down "6" and press combinations of "1" to "5" to zip to another screen!

http://www.engis.co.uk/stuff/hob/index.html

On the main screen, the options include Big or Small display. (amongst other things) There isn't any sound support, sadly. But then, you can't have everything!!
