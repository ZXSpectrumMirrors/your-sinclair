August 1987
Issue 20

Raf Cecco acheives Speccy deity status with Exolon.
    The YS Clapometer makes its first appearance.
    Slots of Fun makes it's debut with reviews of classics OutRun, Rastan Saga, Road Blasters, Super Hang On and GryZor.
    Task Force gives way to extended programming feature Program Pitstop.
    Regular wargames feature On the Warpath starts.
    A certain Tony Worral wins this month's Fanzine of the Month with Eprom.
    Imagine's Game Over advert causes a bit of a stir when the main female villain is pictured with a part of her breast exposed. YS gets around any problems with slapping the company logo over the area.
