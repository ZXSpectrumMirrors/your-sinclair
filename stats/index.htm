A highly eventful year for me, but sadly not for this site!
    The major development for this website is the increased integration with the World Of Spectrum website in that I'm using a lot more graphics that are stored on it. Things like the advert images, the YS cartoon scans, etc, wouldn't have been possible without WOS and its webmaster Martijn var der Heide. The coverage also increased with a significant amount of Letters pages and Adventure columns - stuff which are very text-heavy and took an age to scan.
    One other noticable addition is the integration with The Tipshop, another website I started up with Gerard Sweeney (possibly the biggest YS fan I've ever known), and some people have given us deity status because of the combination!
    But... development of the YSRnRY has been a lot slower in 2001 compared to previous years. I've moved into a new job which I utterly adore and takes up most of my programming energies nowadays. Beyond tinkering every now and then, I've rarely got much spare time for doing stuff on the computer.
    Hey ho. Onto the stats. Raw stats are up again, with both hits and number of visitors doubling since 2000. The "Real" stats are stats gleaned via Thunderstats, which only monitors manual browsers (i.e. it strips out robots and other automated browsers) and give a more accurate report on website usage. You can link to the Thunderstats stats below.
