The Games Machine


ACE

August 1988
Issue 32

Smash Tips collection starts. It was a six-piece booklet which compiled all the tips that had ever been printed in YS up to that point.
    Barbarian II adverts more exposing than the original Barbarian.
    Psycho Pigs UXB goes one better by using a topless model in a bikini.
    Forgotten Worlds, Fighting Soccer and P47 featured in Slots of Fun.
    Jon Ritman and Bernie Drummond interviewed - including details of Starship, "the game that never was".
    T'zers predicts that the first teleprescence coin-op will appear "within the next ten years". Whilst they're wrong, I wouldn't be surprised if it happened by 2010.
